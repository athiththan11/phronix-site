1. Who We Are

Phronix ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile application developed and operated by Athiththan, an individual developer based in Sri Lanka. Phronix helps Sri Lankan consumers identify the best credit or debit card to use at any merchant, based on active bank card offers and promotions.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Phronix collects when you use the app, how it is used, and what rights you have over your data.

By using Phronix, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app and contact us to request deletion of any data we hold.

4. How We Use Your Data

Authentication

Your email address is used solely to create and secure your Phronix account. We use it to identify you across devices and to send account-recovery communications if you request them.

Recommendations

Your card product selections are used exclusively to calculate which of your cards has the best active offer at a given merchant. This processing happens on-device and in our backend; the results are never shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Notifications

Your push notification token is used to send you offer expiry reminders and alerts for offers you have saved. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or in the Phronix Preferences screen.

Product improvement

Aggregated, anonymised app activity data (e.g. which categories are searched most) is used to improve the app. This data cannot identify you individually.

We do not sell your data

Phronix does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, advertiser, or data broker, under any circumstances.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your Phronix account is active.

  • Account deletion: If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts unused for 24 consecutive months will be automatically deleted.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot identify individuals.

To request immediate deletion, contact us at the address in Section 10.

7. Your Rights

Phronix respects the following rights for all users regardless of jurisdiction:

Right to access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time.

Right to correction

You can update your display name and email from the Profile screen. For other corrections, contact us.

Right to deletion

You can delete your account from Profile → Delete account, or by emailing us. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Right to data portability

You can request an export of your data (card list, saved offers, account info) in a machine-readable format by contacting us.

Right to object

You can opt out of analytics data collection at any time by disabling Firebase Analytics in your device settings.

These rights align with the Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022 and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Children

Phronix is intended for users aged 18 and above. The app involves credit and debit card offers, which are financial products not available to minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page, send a push notification to active users, and show an in-app notice on the next launch. Continued use of Phronix after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
